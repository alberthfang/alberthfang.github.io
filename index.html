Hi! I am a Behavioral Scientist at Zillow Group.

My research is in behavioral and psychological science (with applications in markets, business, politics, and public policy), public opinion, and empirical political economy. I also specialize in the design and analysis of experiments, causal inference, applied statistics, and survey methods.

Prior to Zillow, I was a postdoctoral associate at the Yale Center for the Study of American Politics. I received my Ph.D. in political science and a master's in quantitative methods from Columbia University, and I completed my undergraduate studies at the University of California, Berkeley. I also worked in policy evaluation analyzing the impact of large-scale policy interventions for the federal government and philanthropic foundations.

This page contains my academic research and writing.
