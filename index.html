Hi! I am a Research Scientist on the Demography and Survey Science team at Facebook. Previously I was a Behavioral Scientist at Zillow Group and a postdoctoral research associate at the Center for the Study of American Politics at Yale. I have also worked in policy evaluation analyzing the impact of large-scale social policy interventions for governments and philanthropic foundations. My research focuses on behavioral and psychological science (in markets, business, politics, and public policy), public opinion, and empirical political economy. I also specialize in the design and analysis of experiments, causal inference, applied statistics, and survey methods. I hold a Ph.D. in political science from Columbia University and completed undergraduate studies at UC Berkeley.

This page contains my academic research and writing.
